FabLab Internship

This project was a client-focused endeavor where I successfully integrated virtual reality (VR) and the physical world to create a cohesive and immersive experience. The development process included a fascinating feature: the incorporation of my gaming room, which is visible through the window, with a prominent Super Mario display. I implemented a unique functionality using the Quest PRO controller's A-Button, allowing users to seamlessly toggle between passthrough and VR modes. This innovation results in a noticeable and engaging flicker effect, providing users with a clear perspective of the stark difference between the two environments.

MeKiwi Internship

During my MeKiwi internship, I played a pivotal role in developing a dynamic shooting mechanism integrated with hitboxes, powered by advanced raycasting techniques. This innovative approach allowed precise tracking of where the game object was aimed, triggering captivating particle effects to simulate the impact of each shot. My involvement in this project underscores my proficiency in game mechanics and my ability to deliver immersive gameplay experiences.
